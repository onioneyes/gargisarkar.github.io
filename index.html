About

I am a researcher working at the intersection of computer security and criminology, focusing on cybercrimes targeting everyday individuals. My work develops systematic, human-centric policing procedures for cybercrime investigation, with particular emphasis on cyber-enabled human trafficking, forced criminality, digital forensics, and open-source intelligence.

Research Interests

  • Cybercrime investigations and policing systems
  • Digital forensics and electronic evidence analysis
  • Cyber-enabled human trafficking and forced criminality
  • Organized cybercriminal networks
  • OSINT and investigative tooling
  • Cybercrime law, policy, and global governance
